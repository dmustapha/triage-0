[Amoxicillin] (IMCI p.16) 250 mg tablet or 250 mg per 5 ml syrup — give two times daily for 5 days
2 months up to 12 months (4 - <10 kg): 1 tablet or 5 ml
12 months up to 3 years (10 - <14 kg): 2 tablets or 10 ml
3 years up to 5 years (14-19 kg): 3 tablets or 15 ml

[Ciprofloxacin] (IMCI p.16) 250 mg or 500 mg tablet — give 15 mg/kg two times daily for 3 days
Less than 6 months: 1/2 of a 250 mg tablet or 1/4 of a 500 mg tablet
6 months up to 5 years: 1 of a 250 mg tablet or 1/2 of a 500 mg tablet

[Zinc] (IMCI p.23) 20 mg tablet
2 months up to 6 months: 1/2 tablet daily for 14 days
6 months or more: 1 tablet daily for 14 days

[Artemether-lumefantrine] (IMCI p.17) 20 mg artemether and 120 mg lumefantrine tablet — give two times daily for 3 days
5 - <10 kg (2 months up to 12 months): 1 tablet per dose
10 - <14 kg (12 months up to 3 years): 1 tablet per dose
14 - <19 kg (3 years up to 5 years): 2 tablets per dose

[ORS] (IMCI p.23) Plan B — give recommended amount of ORS over 4-hour period
< 6 kg (up to 4 months): 200 - 450 ml
6 - <10 kg (4 months up to 12 months): 450 - 800 ml
10 - <12 kg (12 months up to 2 years): 800 - 960 ml
12 - 19 kg (2 years up to 5 years): 960 - 1600 ml

[Iron] (IMCI p.18) ferrous sulfate + folate tablet (60 mg elemental iron) or ferrous fumarate syrup (100 mg per 5 ml) — give one dose daily for 14 days
2 months up to 4 months (4 - <6 kg): 1.00 ml syrup
4 months up to 12 months (6 - <10 kg): 1.25 ml syrup
12 months up to 3 years (10 - <14 kg): 1/2 tablet or 2.00 ml
3 years up to 5 years (14 - 19 kg): 1/2 tablet or 2.5 ml
