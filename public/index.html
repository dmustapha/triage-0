How it works

Three steps, all on the device

1

Describe the patient

Speak or type the age and what you see. The device turns your voice into text. No recording is sent anywhere.

2

See what the guideline says

It finds the matching step in the WHO guidelines, shows the page it came from, and gives a clear action. It never invents a source.

3

Listen and act

The recommendation is read aloud, so you can keep your hands on the patient. A live readout shows it ran here, not in the cloud.

Why it is different

Built for the places the cloud cannot reach

No network, ever

Every step runs on the laptop. A built-in check proves zero connections leave during a case. The patient story stays private by design.

Grounded in real guidelines

Answers come from the WHO IMCI and mhGAP guides, loaded on the device. Every card shows the page it used, so you can check it.

Proof on the screen

A live readout shows the time to first answer, the speed, and the chip it ran on. The full log is one click away for anyone to check.
